About The Company

Ala Oceano focuses on Enviromentally Friendly Art. Our philosophy is that art can be Beautiful and Benefiting. Beautifully pleaseing to the eye with no compromise to quality with the Benefiting factor of using non-toxic art materials.

Our printed work is done at B Certified printing services with high qualtiy results. Our one of a kind hand made art work is created with ACMI AP approved materials.

About The Artist

Angela Cordon was born, raised, and is currently raisng her own family in San Francisco. From a very young age the art world has always intreguied her, along with the urge to create. Be it art, baking, cooking, or coding she believes there is always more to learn and create.

Most recently she has joined forces with her husband to form, A Vivid Visual. A multi-media design and development company focusing on brand identity, web design and development.
